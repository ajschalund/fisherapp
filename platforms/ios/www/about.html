About Us

We are family owned and operated and are dedicated to providing our community with quality products. We have been serving our community since 1933 and even after 81 years we are still striving to bring the freshest and highest quality products to the community. We support local products and are proud to have a large selection of locally grown produce to choose from. We hope that you will give Hometown Market a chance to be your place to shop for all of your grocery needs.
