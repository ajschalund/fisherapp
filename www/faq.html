Digital Coupons

What are Digital Coupons?
 Digital coupons are just like paper coupons, only easier. They can be loaded directly to your account for later use

How do I claim my coupons?
Log In to the app with your email address and password Go to the coupons section and just tap the coupons that you want to claim. Once claimed to your account you can present them to the cashier at the time of check out.
